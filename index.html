Our Services

Lawn Care

At GreenThumb, we offer comprehensive lawn care services to keep your outdoor space looking its best throughout the year. Our gardening services encompass everything from planting seasonal flowers and shrubs to maintaining vibrant garden beds. With our expert tree trimming services, we ensure that your trees not only enhance the beauty of your landscape but also promote healthy growth. Additionally, we provide weed control, fertilization, and soil testing to nurture your lawn and plants, resulting in a lush and thriving outdoor environment.

Lawn Mowing

Maintaining a well-manicured lawn is essential for enhancing the curb appeal of your property. At GreenThumb, we take pride in offering professional lawn mowing services tailored to your specific needs. Our team of experienced landscapers uses state-of-the-art equipment to ensure precision and efficiency in every mow. Whether you require weekly or bi-weekly mowing, we guarantee a neatly trimmed lawn that will be the envy of your neighborhood.

Leaf Blowing

As the seasons change, leaves accumulate on your lawn, detracting from its pristine appearance. GreenThumb provides efficient leaf blowing services to remove leaves and debris from your property quickly and effectively. Our powerful leaf blowers enable us to clear leaves from lawns, driveways, walkways, and other outdoor areas, leaving behind a clean and tidy landscape. Say goodbye to the hassle of raking leaves by entrusting this task to our skilled team.

Snow Removal

Winter weather can bring challenges for maintaining your property, but GreenThumb is here to help with our reliable snow removal services. Our experienced technicians are equipped with professional-grade snow removal equipment to clear snow and ice from driveways, sidewalks, pathways, and parking areas promptly. We prioritize safety and efficiency to ensure that your property remains accessible and hazard-free during snowy conditions, allowing you to navigate your outdoor space with ease.
